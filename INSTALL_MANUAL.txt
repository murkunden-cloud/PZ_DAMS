2. HOW TO DISTRIBUTE TO CIRCLES (INSTALLATION)
---------------------------------------------------------
There is NO installation required. To set up a Circle office:

1. Send them exactly two files:
   - DAMS.exe
   - config.ini
2. Have them save both files into a single folder (e.g., on their Desktop).
3. Have them open `config.ini` in Notepad.
4. They must set the DataDirectory line to point to their Google Drive folder where their Excel file is kept.
   Example: DataDirectory = C:\Users\Username\Google Drive\DAMS
5. They can now double-click DAMS.exe and log in with their specific Circle User ID.


3. DATA SYNC WORKFLOW (NO MANUAL EXPORTS)
---------------------------------------------------------
Circle users DO NOT need to manually export files or email them to you.

- When a Circle user adds or edits a case in DAMS, the program directly updates the Excel file inside their Google Drive folder.
- Google Drive automatically syncs that file to your computer in the background.

To pull their data into your Master File:
1. Log in to your Admin Dashboard on your computer.
2. Click the "Sync Circle Data" button on the left sidebar.
3. Type in the path to their synced Google Drive Excel file on your PC.
4. Click "Start Sync". The system will automatically inject their new and updated cases into your Master Excel file.


4. UPDATING THE PROGRAM IN THE FUTURE
---------------------------------------------------------
Because the Application (DAMS.exe) and the Data (Excel files) are completely separate, upgrading is incredibly easy.

If you add a new feature or fix a bug in the master program:
1. Double-click the `build_exe.bat` file on your computer.
2. It will generate a brand new `DAMS.exe` file.
3. Simply email the new `DAMS.exe` file to your Circles and tell them to replace their old one.
4. Their data remains 100% untouched and perfectly safe in their Google Drive folder.
